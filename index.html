What we do

At Crest XD, we specialize in data-driven analysis to power the growth of Web3 communities, businesses, and projects. Our expertise spans blockchain technology, decentralized finance (DeFi), and NFT ecosystems, helping you thrive in the decentralized web.

Community and Campaign Analytics

We track community engagement across Web3 platforms, measuring participation, sentiment, and the impact of campaigns in real-time to refine future initiatives and optimize community growth.

Custom Dashboards

We design tailored dashboards that provide seamless access to Web3 data, empowering businesses to make informed decisions with real-time insights into blockchain and DeFi performance.

On-Chain Analytics

We provide in-depth analysis of blockchain data, helping businesses track user behavior, transaction patterns, and overall network health across decentralized networks to make informed decisions.

Predictive Modeling and Research

Using advanced predictive analytics, we provide businesses with forecasts on market trends, user behavior, and campaign outcomes, helping you stay ahead in the evolving Web3 landscape.
