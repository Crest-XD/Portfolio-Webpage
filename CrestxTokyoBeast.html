CrestxD X Tokyo Beast Campaign Dashboard

CREST XD ran a social media campaign to support the Tokyo Beast project. This dashboard tracks the performance of tweets, highlights top contributors, and analyzes key engagement metrics such as likes, retweets, and views.

Top creators were ranked using a custom formula: likes + retweets + log1p(views), which balances visibility and interaction to identify the most impactful posts during the campaign.
